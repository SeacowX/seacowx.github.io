Hi there! I am a (X-2023)-year PhD student at King's College London, under the supervision of Professor Yulan He. My primary research interest is Character-Centric Narrative Understanding, Theory-of-Mind in particular. My study is supported by an EPSRC-iCASE project jointly funded by UKRI and Huawei London Research Centre.

Before KCL, I was a Master's student in the University of Pennsylvania under the supervision of Professor Chris Callison-Burch. Before that, I completed my undergraduate degree in Statistical Data Science at University of California, Davis, where I worked on Functional Data Analysis with Professor Jane-Ling Wang.

Besides research, I worked as an AI Enginner Intern at Schlumberger BGC from 2020 to 2021. I also enjoy reading and playing the clarinet. I was a member of the UC Davis Concert Band and the UC Davis Symphony Orchestra. I am a boardgame enthusiast. I particularly love playing The Resistance: Avalon.

My MBTI is ENFJ (Protagonist).
