Hi there! I am a (X-2023)-year PhD student at King's College London. I am fortunate to be supervised by Professor Yulan He. I am also supervised by Professor Caroline Catmur from KCL's department of psychology and Dr. Jinhua Du from Huawei London Research Centre. My primary research interest is Character-Centric Narrative Understanding, Theory-of-Mind in particular. My study is supported by an EPSRC-iCASE project jointly funded by UKRI and Huawei London Research Centre.

Before KCL, I was a Master's student at the University of Pennsylvania under the supervision of Professor Chris Callison-Burch. During my time at Penn, I worked closely with Li "Harry" Zhang, who is currently a 5-th year PhD student at Penn. Prior to that, I completed my undergraduate degree in Statistical Data Science at University of California, Davis, where I worked on Functional Data Analysis with Professor Jane-Ling Wang.

Besides research, I worked as an AI Enginner Intern at Schlumberger BGC from 2020 to 2021. I enjoy reading detective novels and playing the clarinet. I was a member of the UC Davis Concert Band and the UC Davis Symphony Orchestra. I am also a boardgame enthusiast. I particularly love playing The Resistance: Avalon.

My MBTI is ENFJ (Protagonist).
